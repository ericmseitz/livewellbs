title: "Deadly Shooting on Second Street in Belmont Shore"
date: 2024-03-02
---

Late on the night of Sunday, February 18, 2024, a 32-year-old Long Beach resident, Johnny Santos, was shot and killed in the 5300 block of Second Street — the heart of the Belmont Shore bar and restaurant district. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  

According to police and eyewitness-video posted on social media, what began as a fight between bystanders escalated into gunfire. A group standing outside a local restaurant became embroiled in a physical altercation, and someone then fired a point-blank gunshot. One man collapsed to the ground and later died of his wounds. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  

The shooting sent shockwaves through the community. A resident noted: “Second Street… it’s not looked at as a dangerous place … and there was a ton of people, it was a packed-out Saturday.” :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
