date: 2025-11-02
---

A tragic incident occurred early Sunday morning when a 20-year-old man was stabbed to death during a fight inside Dave’s Hot Chicken on the 5200 block of East Second Street in the heart of the Belmont Shore bar and restaurant district. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Long Beach Police responded around 1:07 a.m. and found the victim suffering an upper-body wound. He succumbed to his injuries at a local hospital. The suspect and additional individuals fled the scene, and investigations are ongoing. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

This marks the second homicide on Second Street in just two weeks, prompting increased security measures and patrols in the area. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
