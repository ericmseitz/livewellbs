## About Live Well Belmont Shore


**Live Well Belmont Shore** is a neighborhood association with a simple goal: **put residents first** in Belmont Shore, Long Beach, CA. We advocate for safe streets, vibrant public spaces, and a community where walking, biking, rolling, and transit are safe and joyful.


- Focus: resident-centered policies, safety, quality of life
- Where: Belmont Shore, Long Beach, California
- How: public meetings, neighborhood listening, working groups, and transparent communication
