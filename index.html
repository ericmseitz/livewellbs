Welcome

We're building a better Shore, by putting residents first.

Our current initiatives are to:

  • Have all 2nd Street bars close at midnight.
  • Require permitted parking throughout the Shore.
  • Require merchants to lock their waste containers.
  • Install engineered traffic calming on all north-south avenues, Ocean, and Toledo.
  • Compliance with and strict enforcement of CA Assembly Bill 413.
